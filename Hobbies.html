My hobbies and Interests

It happened four years back when I was in grade 10. Customizing PC/Mobile Phones

Since I had my hands on android phone, I always loved to customize to improve the performance of the device. Aside from phones, I also like to do same for the laptops as well. Changing the original firmware and replacing it with customized rom that is much faster and better is what I like to do with my devices. But doing so voids the warranty of the device so I always do it after the warranty expires.
