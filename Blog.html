How to Unbrick your mobile phone STEP by STEP

Rijan Bahadur Pradhan, October 7th, 2020

There are two type of brick which are soft brick and hard brick.

Soft bricking means that android device is looping while booting up whereas hard bricking means phone is totally dead without any power. Soft brick can sometimes be easily fixed by factory reset by going to recovery (pressing power and volume combination). But recovering from hard brick can be very difficult.
